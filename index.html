LazerBrick

LEGO has always been a fascination of mine. I find the building process to be stress-relieving, a creative outlet, and if I'm following instructions, a way to feel a sense of completion. I remember going to the store with my Grandmother when I was younger and getting to pick my favorite sets out. Eventually, those sets fell apart and I ran out of money, leading me to build my own creations.

The Heart

Built with pieces on-hand that looked more organic. I then added sharper elements to resemble branching-out vessels. ~ I used three light sources this time, which added some extra challenge to light diffusal. I'm not entirely happy with the end result, but I ran out of time. Nonetheless, it was a fun build.
